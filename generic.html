About

Freelance designer, Garret Gabriel (a.k.a. "gg"), loves for her designs to speak for themselves both eloquently and succinctly. She loves following through with her vision down to the smallest detail. If there's something she does not understand or has not fully conquered she's not afraid to ask questions nor search independently. Further, she very often wants to understand more than is necessary for a project to guarantee a precise, accurate, and catered product.

Not afraid of creative and expressive projects, she began in theatre and some visual art with a degree in Philosophy/Math from Transylvania University, gradually moved into food, beverage, and culinary arts until the second state-wide Covid-19 dining room closure, then finally married her art and rational backgrounds in a coding class, Commonwealth Coders--completed in May 2022.

She hopes you can find some website design styles that can serve as potential guides to what her frontend and backend knowledge can offer you as a potential consumer. Feel comfortable contacting her directly if you see something of interest. Welcome to the portfolio!
